The Village Dungeon

This is the Village Dungeon, where Jharod, the healer and Yrrigs, the mad carpenter can be found. It is only available after obtaining a quest from Rynt, the village elder in Terinyo. See section 1.2.1.2.1 for more about the Village Dungeon.
