The northern tunnel contains ghuls, skeletons, zombies, shadows, wights and wraiths. Ghosts may pass through the walls into this corridor. The central room contains, in addition, more dangerous undead: corpse fiends, ghosts, lichs, mummies and greater mummies, skeletal warriors, spectres and vampires. The monsters here who see invisible are: mummies, greater mummies and lichs.

The graves are diggable. See section 2.7 concerning digging up graves. See Appendix J for monster characteristics.
